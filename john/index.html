John Speelman

John Speelman was a craftsman, builder, and the father of David Speelman. His most iconic project — a model Spanish galleon — was constructed over many years, beginning in the early 1960s and completed in 1979. It stands as a testament to his creativity, persistence, and skill.

While we don't have an interview with John himself, his son David shares the story of the galleon and reflects on John's life and influence in a recorded conversation.
